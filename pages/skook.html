Mr. Skookum

adjective: (of a person or animal) strong, brave, or impressive.

noun: a street party or trade fair.

He was all of those things and more! Skookum was my life companion from October 19, 2014 - October 22, 2021. The first pet I ever owned on my own. We found eachother when he was just 4 months old at our local shelter in Idaho. We moved and traveled all over together. He moved with me to Los Angeles and back, we had many adventures in California, Utah, Idaho and spent most of our summers up in the alpine lakes of the Sawtooth Mountains, either hiking and camping. He left this realm very quickly due to cancer and unexpectedly passed over the rainbow bridge. This page is dedicated to him and our life together.

Cheers Mr. Skookum - May we meet again one day
