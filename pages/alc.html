AIDS/LifeCycle

2015 - 2019 & BEYOND

AIDS/LifeCycle is a 7 day, 545-mile bike ride from San Francisco to Los Angeles, co-produced by and benefiting San Francisco AIDS Foundation and the Los Angeles LGBT Center. We call it the LOVE BUBBLE! A Life Changing Experience! Over 2,500 cyclists, 500 roadies/volunteers! The ride and everyone involved raises millions each year that help people living with HIV/AIDS get the care they need. We will ride until HIV/AIDS is a thing of the past!
